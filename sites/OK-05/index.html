What is DisasterReady?

Preparing for disasters,
before they happen

DisasterReady is an AI-assisted web application that helps families and individuals build real disaster preparedness — not just awareness. It pulls live data from weather services, FEMA, and the National Weather Service to give you a real-time picture of risk in your area, then guides you through personalized emergency plans, supply checklists, evacuation routes, and household management tools. Everything is built for reliability it's available exactly when you need it most.

Key Features

Real-Time Alerts

Custom plans for earthquakes, floods, wildfires, and hurricanes.
